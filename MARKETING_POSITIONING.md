# UMKM Core - Marketing & Positioning Strategy

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Date:** December 3, 2025
**Target Market:** Indonesian Small-Medium Businesses (UMKM)

## Executive Summary

UMKM Core should be positioned as a **simple business management app** for Indonesian small businesses, NOT as a full ERP system. The target users are non-technical business owners who need basic tools for POS, stock tracking, and cash management.

## 5. Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model (Recommended)

**FREE TIER (Gratis Selamanya):**
- 1 toko, 1 user
- 50 produk
- 100 transaksi/bulan
- Laporan dasar (basic reports)
- **Purpose:** Lower barrier to entry, let UMKM test risk-free

**PRO PLAN: Rp 99.000/bulan (~$7 USD)**
- 1 toko, 5 users
- Unlimited produk
- Unlimited transaksi
- Laporan lengkap
- Support WhatsApp
- **Target:** Single-location small businesses

**BUSINESS PLAN: Rp 199.000/bulan (~$14 USD)**
- 3 toko (multi-location)
- Unlimited users
- Priority support
- Custom reports
- **Target:** Growing businesses with multiple outlets

### Why This Pricing Works

- Rp 99k/month = cost of 2-3 coffee shop visits
- Much cheaper than traditional POS systems (millions Rp upfront)
- Monthly subscription = low commitment, easy to cancel
- Still profitable for SaaS business model

## 8. Launch Strategy (First 100 Customers)

### Phase 1: Beta Launch (Weeks 1-4)

**Target:** 10-20 pilot users (friends, family, network)

**Actions:**
- Offer free Pro plan for 6 months
- Weekly feedback sessions (WhatsApp group)
- Iterate based on real usage patterns
- Document pain points and feature requests

**Success Metrics:**
- All beta users complete onboarding
- At least 70% use the app weekly
- Collect 5+ video testimonials

---

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Weeks 5-12)

**Target:** 50 paying customers (1 specific niche)

**Focus:** Coffee shops in Bandung/Jakarta

**Actions:**
- Launch promo: "3 months Pro plan for Rp 50k" (50% off)
- Partner with coffee roaster (they introduce you to cafes)
- Create WhatsApp group for all coffee shop users
- Run Instagram ads targeting "pemilik cafe" + location

**Success Metrics:**
- 50 signups in 8 weeks
- 30% conversion from free to Pro plan
- 10+ customer testimonials collected

---

### Phase 3: Public Launch (Week 13+)

**Target:** 100 total paying customers

**Actions:**
- Launch website with 20+ testimonials
- Run Google Ads (keywords: "aplikasi kasir umkm")
- Partner with UMKM influencers (Instagram/TikTok)
- Launch special: "50% off first 3 months for first 100 customers"
- Press release to tech/business media

**Success Metrics:**
- 100 paying customers by Month 6
- 20% MoM growth rate
- Less than 5% monthly churn rate

### Visual Identity Guidelines

**Colors:**
- Primary: Warm, approachable (Indonesian-friendly)
- Avoid: Too corporate (blue/gray), too playful (bright pink)
- Suggested: Teal/Green (growth, money) or Orange (energetic, friendly)

**Icons:**
- Simple, recognizable (not abstract/modern)
- Use everyday metaphors (cash register, box/package, graph)

**UI Design:**
- WhatsApp-like familiarity (green, rounded corners)
- Large touch targets (minimum 44x44px)
- Minimize typing, maximize tapping

## 12. Sales Objection Handling

### Common Objections & Responses

**Objection 1:** "Saya sudah pakai Excel, kenapa harus pakai aplikasi?"
**Response:** "Excel bagus, tapi kalau mau cepat dan otomatis, aplikasi lebih praktis. Tinggal klik, data langsung terupdate. Hemat waktu 1-2 jam per hari."

**Objection 2:** "Mahal, saya bisnis kecil kok"
**Response:** "Rp 99.000 sebulan = harga 2 kali makan siang. Tapi manfaatnya hemat waktu hitung stok, gak ada salah hitung, laporan otomatis. Worth it!"

**Objection 3:** "Saya gak ngerti teknologi"
**Response:** "Makanya kami buat sesimpel mungkin. Kalau bisa pakai WhatsApp, pasti bisa pakai ini. Ada video tutorial dan support kami siap bantu."

**Objection 4:** "Kalau internet mati gimana?"
**Response:** "Data tersimpan di cloud jadi aman. Untuk transaksi, bisa pakai mode offline (coming soon). Plus, modal HP sekarang bisa hotspot kan?"

**Objection 5:** "Nanti ribet migrasi data"
**Response:** "Kami bantu import data dari Excel Anda. Tim support kami guide step by step. Paling lama setup 1 hari, setelah itu lancar."

## 13. Success Metrics (KPIs to Track)

### Acquisition Metrics

- **Website visitors** (monthly)
- **Free signups** (per week)
- **Trial-to-paid conversion rate** (target: 20-30%)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** (target: < Rp 200k per customer)

### Activation Metrics

- **Onboarding completion rate** (target: >80%)
- **Time to first transaction** (goal: <10 minutes)
- **Products added in first week** (goal: >5 products)

### Engagement Metrics

- **Daily/Weekly/Monthly Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU)**
- **Average session duration** (goal: >5 minutes)
- **Transactions per user per day** (goal: >10)

### Retention Metrics

- **Monthly churn rate** (goal: <5%)
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** (goal: >100%)
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** (target: Rp 2.4 juta+)

### Satisfaction Metrics

- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** (goal: >50)
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)** (goal: >4.5/5)
- **Support ticket resolution time** (goal: <24 hours)
